Frequently asked

What is a digital vehicle health check?

A digital vehicle health check (VHC) is a structured inspection carried out on a tablet or phone instead of paper, where a technician marks each item (brakes, tyres, fluids, lights, and so on) as pass, advisory, or fail, attaches photos of anything needing attention, and generates a report the customer can review before authorising further work.

Does GarageDash include digital vehicle health checks?

Yes. Digital vehicle health checks are included in GarageDash's standard £29/month subscription, with no extra fee per inspection or per photo. Technicians can complete checks from a tablet in the workshop, and the system produces a printable customer report automatically.

Can customers see photos of the fault before approving work?

Yes. Each advisory or fail item can have photos attached, so the customer report shows exactly what the technician found alongside the pass/advisory/fail rating, which helps build trust and reduces disputes over recommended work.
