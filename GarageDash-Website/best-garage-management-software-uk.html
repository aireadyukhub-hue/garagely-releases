Best garage management software UK (2026): 6 options compared

A sourced roundup of the garage management systems UK independent garages actually choose between, what each one costs, and who each is really built for.

Last updated 6 July 2026. Pricing is publicly reported and can change — sources linked throughout, confirm directly with the vendor before deciding.

Who's writing this: GarageDash is one of the six options below — we make garage management software ourselves, so we're not a neutral third party. That said, every price, contract term and feature claim here is sourced from each vendor's own published pricing page or a named third-party listing, linked so you can check it yourself. We've tried to be straight about where other options are the better fit.

Quick answer

If you're an independent garage or small workshop that wants the lowest total cost with no per-user fees, no setup fee and no contract, GarageDash (£29/month) fits best. If you run a larger or multi-site operation and need deep reporting, TechMan or Garage Hive are more established, at a higher price. If you service commercial or fleet vehicles, GDS's Commercial Vehicle Manager is built for that. If you sell used cars as well as repair them, Dragon2000 covers stock/retail on top of workshop management. AutoChain is a similarly simple, low-contract option to GarageDash at a somewhat higher published price (£69.99/month + VAT).

Frequently asked

What is the best garage management software in the UK for independent garages?

For independent garages and small workshops prioritising low cost and simplicity, GarageDash (£29/month per garage, unlimited users, no contract) is the strongest fit. TechMan and Garage Hive suit larger or multi-site operations that need deeper reporting and can absorb higher costs. GDS suits workshops that also handle commercial/fleet vehicles. Dragon2000 suits car dealerships selling stock as well as running a workshop. AutoChain is a simple, low-cost option similar in spirit to GarageDash, at a somewhat higher published price.

Is there a completely free garage management software?

None of the dedicated UK garage management systems compared here are free ongoing, but most (GarageDash, Dragon2000, AutoChain) offer a free trial period before you pay anything, and GarageDash's trial requires no card upfront.

Which garage management software has no long-term contract?

GarageDash and AutoChain both operate with no minimum term — cancel any time. TechMan is commonly sold on 3-year terms, and Garage Hive requires an initial 6-month term. GDS and Dragon2000 contract terms are not published and vary by quote.
