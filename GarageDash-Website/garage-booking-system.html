Frequently asked

What is a garage booking system?

A garage booking system is software that shows every upcoming booking and job in a visual calendar, so a garage can see gaps, avoid double-booking a bay or technician, and turn a confirmed booking straight into a job sheet without re-entering the customer or vehicle details.

Does GarageDash include a booking calendar?

Yes. GarageDash's booking calendar is included in the standard £29/month subscription and shows all bookings and jobs in one visual view, with technician scheduling and time-off tracking, so front-desk staff can see availability at a glance.

Can a booking turn into a job sheet automatically?

Yes. In GarageDash, a booking is tied to the same customer and vehicle record used for job sheets, quotes and invoices, so confirming a booking creates the job sheet directly rather than requiring the details to be typed in twice.
