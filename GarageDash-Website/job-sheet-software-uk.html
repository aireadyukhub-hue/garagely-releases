Frequently asked

What is job sheet software?

Job sheet software replaces paper job cards with a digital record of each vehicle repair or service: what work is booked, which technician is assigned, what parts and labour are used, and the job's status from booked through to invoiced, all in one place instead of scattered across paper sheets.

Can multiple technicians use the same job sheet system?

Yes. GarageDash is priced per garage rather than per user, so the whole team, including a simplified Technician Mode for workshop staff, can use the same job sheet system under one subscription with no per-seat fees.

Does job sheet software handle UK VAT on invoices?

GarageDash's job sheets flow straight into quotes and invoices with UK VAT handling built in, so there's no separate step to add VAT correctly when a job is completed and billed.
