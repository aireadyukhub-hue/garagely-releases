Questions

Frequently asked questions

What is GarageDash?

GarageDash is garage management software for UK garages and independent mechanics. It covers bookings, job sheets, quotes, invoices, customer and vehicle records, parts stock, and digital vehicle-health-check inspections, on desktop (Mac and Windows) and in the browser.

How much does GarageDash cost?

GarageDash is £29 per month per garage, with unlimited users included. There are no per-technician or per-seat fees, unlike most garage management systems.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. GarageDash offers a 14-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel any time before then.

Does GarageDash work on Mac and Windows?

Yes. GarageDash has a native Windows app (Mac coming soon), plus a browser-based web app that works on any device, including tablets in the workshop.

Can my whole team use GarageDash, or is it per user?

GarageDash is priced per garage, not per user — your whole team can use it under one £29/month subscription. There's also a Technician Mode that gives staff a simplified view (bookings, job sheets, customers, vehicles, inspections) without pricing or reporting.

Who is GarageDash built for?

Independent garages, mobile mechanics, and small workshops in the UK who want an affordable, easy-to-use alternative to enterprise garage management software.
