## Per-site notes

### SoftwareSuggest
- [softwaresuggest.com/vendors](https://www.softwaresuggest.com/vendors) → free listing, choose
  "Software Profile" when it asks Company/Software/Service Profile.
- Free listing tier exists; they'll try to upsell a paid "featured" placement — free is fine to
  start. Note: their guidelines say listed software should be pre-built/off-the-shelf (GarageDash
  qualifies) rather than custom/bespoke.
- Asks for category — choose **Garage Management Software** if offered, otherwise **Field Service
  Management** or **Automotive Software**.
- They may ask for a demo video link — skip if none exists yet, not required.

### Capterra (and GetApp, Software Advice — same parent company, Gartner Digital Markets)
- [capterra.com/vendors](https://www.capterra.com/vendors/) (UK: capterra.co.uk/company/vendors) →
  free product listing for vendors, sign up from there. One submission here typically also gets you
  considered for GetApp and Software Advice since they share a backend, though each may need its
  own confirmation step.
- Category: **Auto Repair Software** or **Garage Management Software**.
- These sites weight **reviews heavily** in their internal ranking — the free listing gets you
  found, but ranking within the category page depends on review count, so this is worth revisiting
  once there are a handful of real customers to prompt for a review.
- Pricing must be entered as a structured field (not just prose) — use "Starting price: £29.00,
  Pricing model: Per user (or per garage, if their taxonomy allows), Free trial: Yes, 14 days".

### Software Advice
- Covered by the Capterra vendor sign-up above in most cases — check after submitting to Capterra
  whether it's already picked up; if not, softwareadvice.com has its own vendor contact form.

### G2
- [g2.com/products/new](https://www.g2.com/products/new) → submit the product; G2's team verifies
  and categorises it within a few business days before the profile goes live and can be claimed.
  Alternatively [sell.g2.com/create-a-profile](https://sell.g2.com/create-a-profile) for the vendor
  side.
- G2 leans even harder on reviews for visibility than Capterra — the listing itself is easy, ranking
  well takes review volume over time.
- Category: **Auto Repair Software** (closest existing G2 category as of writing — check for a more
  specific "Garage Management" category if G2 has added one).

## After submitting

Once live on any of these, it's worth linking to the GarageDash listing from `compare.html` or the
footer as social proof, and asking early customers who've had a good experience to leave a review on
whichever directory the garage found you through — reviews compound review-site rankings, GBP
rankings, and organic trust signals all at once, so it's the single highest-leverage thing left once
listings exist.
