# GarageDash — Brand Brief (paste this into Canva AI along with the images)

## What GarageDash is
Garage management software for independent UK garages — bookings, job sheets, digital inspections, quotes → invoices, MOT/service reminders, and a free reg (VRM) check. Web app + technician mode for the workshop floor. **£29/month, 14-day free trial, no card needed. garagedash.co.uk**

## Colours (use exactly)
- Accent gold: **#F4A523** — buttons, highlights, the "DASH" in the wordmark
- Void black: **#0A0A0A** — main backgrounds
- Lift black: **#111111** — cards/panels
- Seam: **#1E1E1E** — borders/dividers
- Smoke: **#999999** — secondary text
- White: **#FFFFFF** — headlines

## Typography
Inter (or closest available). Big bold white headlines, grey supporting text, gold pill-shaped CTA buttons with black text.

## Logo rules
- `logo-on-dark-bg.png` — full logo for dark backgrounds (preferred; the brand is dark-first)
- `logo-on-light-bg.png` — full logo for light backgrounds
- `logo-mark-hexagon.png` — the hexagon G mark alone (small spaces, avatars)
- `logo-badge-any-bg.png` — self-contained badge, safe on any/busy background
- Keep clear space around the logo; don't recolour it, add shadows, or stretch it.

## Look & feel
Dark, premium, workshop-tough. Near-black backgrounds with a subtle warm glow, one gold accent — never multiple bright colours. See the `example-ad-*.png` files for the exact style to match.

## Voice
Plain-spoken, confident, a bit dry. Written for busy UK garage owners — no corporate jargon, no hype. Short punchy headlines ("Bin the scribbled checklist."). British English. Always end with the trial line: "14-day free trial · no card needed · garagedash.co.uk"
